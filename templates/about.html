03 : About me

Who Am I?

My name is Jay Michalek. I currently work with Cognizant as a Full Time Healthcare Technology Digital Developer in Phoenix, AZ. I learned various programming languages during my journey. So far, my favorites are Python and SQL.

My background...

I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Software Development from Western Governor's University in December 2021. While going to school, I was working as a nurse manager in various skilled nursing facilities. I was a licensed practical nurse for many years. However, when I came across the terms "software development" for the first time (yes, for the first time) in 2017, I instantly fell in love with the idea of building something from nothing! A simple idea that could come to life in the software realm is awesome!

Life Goals!..

I wish to combine my acquired nursing experience and software development skills so that I may offer value and be part of a rewarding team. Ultimately, I am aiming to build seamless software solutions for real world healthcare problems.
